Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: Плов
Ingredients Quantity
мясо 2000g
рис 500g
морковь 200g
лук 200g

Step 1: In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat..

Step 2: Add chopped onions and minced garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

Step 3: Add diced carrots and continue cooking for another 3 minutes.

Step 4: Add the lamb and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.

Step 5: Stir in chickpeas, cumin, paprika, coriander, and turmeric. Cook for 2 minutes.

Step 6: Rinse rice and add it to the saucepan with 2 cups of water. Stir to combine.

Step 7: Increase heat to high and bring the mixture to a boil.

Step 8: Reduce heat to low, cover, and let cook for about 20 minutes or until rice is tender and all the liquid is absorbed.

Step 9: Stir gently, taste, and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary.

Step 10: Serve the plov hot, garnished with chopped cilantro.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: Манты
Ingredients Quantity
мясо 2000g
тесто 500g
морковь 200g
лук 200g

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix together flour, egg, salt, pepper, cumin, coriander, and paprika.

Step 2: Gradually add water and mix until a soft dough forms.

Step 3: Divide the dough into 12-16 equal parts and roll each one into a ball.

Step 4:On a floured surface, roll out each ball of dough into a thin round disk.

Step 5: Heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook each disk of dough for 1-2 minutes on each side

Step 6: In a separate pan, heat oil over medium heat.

Step 7:Add chopped onions and minced garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

Step 8: Add the ground beef or lamb and cook until browned, about 5 minutes..

Step 9: Stir in chopped carrots, potatoes, and tomatoes.

Step 10: Add 4 cups of water, bring to a boil, reduce heat and let simmer for 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 0.30 hour
  • Serves: Макароны
Ingredients Quantity
макароны 2000g

Step 1:Fill a large pot with 8 quarts of water and bring to a boil over high heat.

Step 2: Add 2 tablespoons of salt to the boiling water.

Step 3: Once the water is boiling, add the pasta to the pot.

Step 4: Stir the pasta occasionally to prevent it from sticking together.

Step 5:Cook the pasta until al dente, following the instructions on the package. This usually takes 7-9 minutes for spaghetti and 9-11 minutes for fettuccine.

Step 6: Once the pasta is cooked, drain it in a colander and rinse under cold running water to stop the cooking process.

Step 7: If desired, drizzle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil to prevent the pasta from sticking together.

Step 8: Serve the pasta hot with your favorite sauce. Enjoy!
